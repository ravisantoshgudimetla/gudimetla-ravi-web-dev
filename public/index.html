FOTOTAG

Fototag is social networking site, which is used for anonymous searching of user based of photo. If a user is registered with this website, he/she can anonymously search against the database of users based on their profile. The user also has the capability to integrate his Facebook account with this website, which makes this even more interesting as he/she can search against every user registered with Facebook.

This can be used in plethora of situations like emergencies or national disasters where a user can be searched against fototagâ€™s database which can reveal his friends and help them in knowing the information about them. Every year thousands of children are misplaced and taken away from parents. Fototag can help people who work in non-government organizations to identify the children and help them in safely returning them to parents.
